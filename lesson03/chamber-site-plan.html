Cape Town Chamber of Commerce


Site Name

The domain name I chose is http://capetownchamberofcommerce.co.za/

I chose this name because I thought it would help get the point across to the viewer. Additionally, using the co.za instead of .com gives the site a unique identifier that links back to its area of origin.


Site Purpose

To assist firms and individuals interested in investing in Cape Town in becoming aware of the conditions influencing the city and deciding on a course of action that will best promote economic and social growth.


Scenarioes

  • A businessman is looking for an investment opportunity in Cape Town. He will require a fundamental understanding of Cape Town business practises.

  • A foreigner is looking for employment opportunities in Cape Town. He or she will require details on which businesses are employing and how much they compensate their employees.

  • A company wants to open a branch in Cape Town. They will need to be aware of the regulations to follow and the spaces that are available to locate their site.
